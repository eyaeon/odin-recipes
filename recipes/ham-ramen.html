Ponyo's Ham Ramen

Description

An instant ham ramen recipe from the Studio Ghibli film, Ponyo.

Difficulty: Very easy

Time: 3 mins

Servings: 3

Ingredients

  • 1 package Nissin Chicken Ramen
  • 400ml boiling water
  • 1 slice cooked ham (cut in half)
  • 1/2 hard boiled egg
  • chopped green onion

Steps

  1. Boil the water.
  2. Place the noodles in a ramen bowl. Pour boiling water over the noodles and cover. Set and start your timer for three minutes
  3. After the first minute, quickly open, shake the noodles to separate, then arrange the ham, egg, and green onion over the noodles.
  4. Cover again and waituntil the timer beeps.
