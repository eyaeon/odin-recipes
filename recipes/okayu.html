Princess Mononoke's Okayu

Description

A rice porrige recipe as seen from the Studio Ghibli film, Princess Mononoke.

Ingredients

  • 2cups white rice
  • 6 cups water
  • 3 tbsp dashi miso paste
  • Soy saucce
  • 4 eggs
  • Basil, diced
  • Hot chili or sesame oil

Steps

  1. Add both the water and rice to a pot over high heat.
  2. Once boiling, cover the pot and put the heat on low. Letit simmer for about 40 minutes until it turns soft andporridge-like.
  3. Add the miso paste and a splash of soy sauce (to taste) and mix over low heat until the miso paste is dissolved - about 5 minutes.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs until light and fluffy, then pour into the rice bowl.
  5. Stir to combine and take off the heat. After serving into 4 bowls garnish each with sliced basil and hot chili oil and/or sesame oil.
  6. Serve into 4 bowls and garnish with basil and hot chili oil and/or sesame oil.
